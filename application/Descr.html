DESCRIPTION

"The term business-to-consumer (B2C) refers to the process of selling products and services directly between a business and consumers who are the end-users of its products or services. Most companies that sell directly to consumers can be referred to as B2C companies". The term B2C is applicable to any business transaction where the consumer directly receives goods or services -- such as retail stores, restaurants and doctor's offices. Most often it refers to e-commerce businesses, which use online platforms to connect their products with consumers.
